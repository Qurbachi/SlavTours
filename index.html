Meet our crew

Andriey Jurovskyi

Co-founder of the Slavengers

I come from Ivanofrankivsk in Ukraine. Travelling has been my passion since I remeber. Although I travell all over the world I try to rediscover my region all the time. I live in Cracow in Poland on a daily basis. I`m going to take You on a tour to Chernobyl zone and many other interesting destinations in Ukraine, Belarus and Poland.

Henryk Szewczyk

The most experienced man in our team

I have lived in communism for the most of my life so I know how to prevent others from that system. I also spent a couple of years in Romania as a bussiness advisor. I don`t feel like retiring and doing trips make me a great pleasure. I will acompany You on tours in former the USSR`s republics, starting form Estonia and ending on Ukraine.

Damian Tworzydło

Founder of the Slavengers

I decided to give foreigners a real experience of slavic life and culture. I got bored of repetitive offer of local tourist offices. I want You not only to visit our region and see some picturesque landmarks and main attractions. I really wish You understood the background of our culture, got know the history deeply and visit some places that guidebooks don`t say a word about. I will show You around various polish regions and Baltic states.1x,

Cornel Petrescu

Romanian in the slavic world

I went on Erasmus exchange program to Poland and I stayed there for a bit longer. People know stereotypes about Romania, but they do not know Romania as it schould be known. I`m going to take You on a tour to my homeland and show it to You like You couldn`t expect.
